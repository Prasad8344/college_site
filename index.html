Admission

Overview Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University is inviting applications for admission to its Degree Programs in various streams. Admission will be on merit-basis. Application form can be filled online or can be obtained from university campus. The candidates are required to fill up the application form along with all the mandatory documents

Academics

Academics of DSU is, essentially multidisciplinary, offering four major streams initially: Medicine, Paramedical, Engineering & Technology and Agricultural Sciences. A combination of School of Medicine and School of Allied Health sciences complement each other. In addition, School of Physiotherapy and College of Nursing posits Health Education holistic in DSU. Engineering & Technology stream is offering host of eleven Engineering programs starting from B.E Mechanical Engineering, Chemical Engineering to cutting edge branches like B.E Artificial Intelligence and Data Sciences to Cyber Security.
